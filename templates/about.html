About Kansi AI

Kansi AI was built with one belief: technology should be kind. We use machine learning to gently identify emotional patterns in text, not to label anyone, but to open a door to awareness and support.

How It Works

You write freely. Kansi AI reads the emotional texture of your words, looking for linguistic patterns like emotional tone, self-focused language, and expressions that research has linked to distress. It is like having a gentle observer who notices what you might not see yourself.

The Science Behind It

Our model uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) to understand which words and phrases carry emotional weight. It was trained using supervised machine learning and evaluated rigorously.

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. Passwords are salted and hashed. Analysis is processed locally. We do not share, sell, or expose your information. You can sign in with your email or Google account, whatever feels comfortable.

Our Promise

Kansi AI will never pretend to be a therapist. It is a screening tool, a first step, a gentle nudge, a way to check in with yourself. For real support, we always encourage speaking with a professional who can truly understand your situation.

If something in your results ever concerns you, please know that help is always available. In the UK, you can call Samaritans at 116 123 (free, 24/7). Worldwide, visit findahelpline.com to find support near you.
